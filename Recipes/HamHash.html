Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole

This dish is easy to make and quick to bake. Make it for dinner or have it as a side. Great for people who are wanting to eat healthier meals.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1 (32 ounce) package of frozen hash brown potatoes
  • 2 (10.5 ounch) cans of condensed cream of potatoe soup
  • 1 (16 ounce) container of sour cream
  • 2 cups of shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 8 ounces of cooked, diced ham
  • 1 1/2 cups of grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish wiht cooking spray
  2. Step 2: Mix together the hash browns, condensed soup, sour cream, Cheddar cheese, and ham in a bowl until well combined. Spread evenly into the prepared dish. Sprinkle with Parmnesan cheese.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven unil bubbly and lightly brown, about 1 hour.
