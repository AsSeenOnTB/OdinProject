Black Eyed Pea Soup

This classic dish is perfect as a holdiday dish. It also makes a great dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pork sausage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cups of water
  • 3 )15 ounce) cans black-eyed peas, drained
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10 ounce) can of diced tomatoes with green chile peppers(such as Rotel brand), undrained
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (4 ounce) can of chopped green chillies
  • 4 teaspoons molasses
  • 4 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions

  1. Step 1: In a large Dutch oven, cook and stir the pork sausage adn ground beef with the onion over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink, 10 to 12 minutes; drain of any excess fat. Pour in the water, and stir in black-eyed peas, diced tomatoes, tomatoes with green chiles, Worcestershire sauce, garlic salt, salt, canned green chhilies, molasses, beef bouillon cubes, black pepper, and cumin until thouroughly mixed.
  2. Step 2: Bring the soup to a boil, redice heat to a simmer, cover and simmer for 45 minutes.
