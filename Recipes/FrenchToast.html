Overngiht Blueberry French Toast

This French Toast is made with blueberries, making it part of a balanced breakfast!

The blueberries add a nice flavor, something I'm not used to

This recipe can be long, but once you bite into it, the wait will be worth it

Ingredients

  • 12 Slices of day-old bread, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2(8 ounce) packages of cream cheese, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 cup of fresh blueberries
  • 12 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup

For the Blueberry Sauce

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 Tablespoons of Corn Starch
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Step 1: Prepare casserole: LIghtly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 1/2 of the bread cubes in the dish and top with cream cheese cubes, then top with remaining bread cubes.
  2. Step 2: Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and syrup together in a large bowl until well-comined; pour over bread cubes. Cover and refirgerate overnight.
  3. Step 3: Remove casserole fromt refridgerator about 30 minutes before baking. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 C).
  4. Step 4: Bake casserile in the preheated oven, covered, for 30 minutes. Uncover, and continue naking until center is firm and surfafce is llightly browned, about 25 to 20 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Meanwhile, prepare blueberry, reduce heat to low, and simmer until all the blueberries, burst, about 10 minutes. Sir the butter.
  6. Step 6: Serve portions of casserole on plates and pour warm syrup over the top.
